Possible Discussion Questions:

  1. Despite our best efforts, prejudice seeps into our worldviews. How can we best combat it?
  2. What is something each of us can do in the next week/month to increase understanding or dissolve race barriers?
  3. What experiences have shaped your own views on prejudice and race?
  4. Is there something we could do on the community level?
  5. What can we do as residents of our neighborhoods and of Chicago?
  6. How would we like the national discussion of race to shift? What is overlooked?
